Course Projects

Your grade in this course will be based on a small project, which you will hand in as a report of a maximum of 10 pages, and about which you will make a  10 minute presentation during the final week of the term.

The goal of this project is to convince yourself that you have gained a working understanding of some aspect of molecular simulation that is of interest to you. For this reason, you are given a large amount of freedom in choosing your topic. Here you'll find a compiled list of suggested project exercises, also found interspersed throughout the rest of the website.

You'll also see the schedule. Sign-up is first-come, first-served. Please send me an email at cfa2@drexel.edu with your project title and your top three time-slot choices as soon as possible. There are 13 available slots as of May 14, 2004; as there are 10 of you officially giving presentations, this should be adequate.
